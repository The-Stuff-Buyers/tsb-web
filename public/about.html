About Us

Who We Are

The Stuff Buyers LLC is a wholesale inventory acquisition and liquidation brokerage based in Texas. We connect businesses that have excess, surplus, overstock, discontinued, closeout, or unwanted inventory with buyers who will purchase that inventory in bulk.

Think of us as the bridge between a company that has a warehouse full of product they need to move and a nationwide network of auction and resale buyers ready to buy it. We broker the deal and make the connection as fast and painless as possible for the seller.

Our Network

Our Partner: BidFTA

BidFTA (Fast Track Auctions) is our primary liquidation auction partner. They operate a nationwide online auction marketplace with 30+ warehouse locations, specializing in liquidation, returns, overstock, and surplus inventory. Their buyer network includes resellers, small businesses, bargain retailers, and bin store operators.

When inventory enters our pipeline, BidFTA evaluates it and provides pricing. We review and relay the offer to the seller. All communication between the seller and BidFTA flows through us — we protect seller relationships at every step.

The Workflow

How It Works

Every deal follows the same straightforward process. Getting a quote is free, fast, and comes with zero obligation.

01

Submit Your Inventory

Tell us what you have — product type, quantity, condition, and location. Submit via our website intake form, email, or in person.

02

We Review & Send to Our Auction Network

Our team reviews your submission and sends the inventory details to BidFTA for evaluation and pricing.

03

You Receive an Offer

BidFTA responds with a price, timeline, and conditions. We review it for quality, then send it to you.

04

You Decide

Accept the offer and we coordinate the rest. Decline, and there's no cost or obligation. Simple as that.
